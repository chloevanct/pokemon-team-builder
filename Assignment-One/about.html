CPSC 455: Assignment 1

As a student in UBC CPSC 455 during the Summer of 2024, I have created a team builder website for Assignment 1. The purpose of this website is to utilize Vanilla JS, HTML, and CSS which we covered in Workshop 1.

Students can register a member into a team, read about other members, and remove individual members. Additionally, the website is designed to be responsive across most devices as tested in Chrome's developer tools.
